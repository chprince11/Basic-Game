Assignment 1 - Prince Chauhan

Game Instructions:

  • First enter any operator( (+), (-) , and (*) ) of your choice.
  • Then enter the correct answer for the given expression.
  • If you do right answer, then you will get 1 point for the correct answer and if wrong, then also you will get 1 incorrect point.
  • You can play as many times as you wish.
  • If you want to exit the game, just click on cancel option when it ask that Do you want to play again!
  • At the end you will get a score board of your played gameplay. Best of luck! Have fun😊
  • Click on START to play the game!
